# 11. Workflow Automation

## No-code Workflow Builder
- Drag-and-drop steps
- Conditions (if/else)
- Approval matrix
- Timers and escalation rules

## Notifications
- Email, Teams, Slack
- Per-event templates
- SLA breach alerts

## Examples
- Auto assign based on service + site
- Auto close after requester confirmation
- Escalate to manager if SLA breach risk > 80%
