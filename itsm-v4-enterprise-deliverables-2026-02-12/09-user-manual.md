# User Manual

## Access
- Login via SSO or username and password.
- Use MFA when prompted for higher risk actions.

## Incident Management
- Create incident with impact and urgency.
- Track status and SLA target.
- Add attachments and evidence.
- Confirm resolution to close the ticket.

## Service Requests
- Browse service catalog and submit request.
- Provide required form inputs.
- Track approvals and fulfillment status.

## Knowledge Base
- Search articles by service or category.
- Rate articles and suggest improvements.

## Notifications
- Receive email or WhatsApp notifications (if enabled).
- Configure notification preferences in profile.

## Reporting
- View personal and team dashboards.
- Export permitted reports (PDF/Excel).
